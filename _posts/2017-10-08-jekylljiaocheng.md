### jekyll中插入图片
插入图片:
```
![image-title-here](/path/to/image.jpg){:class="img-responsive"}
```

控制图片高度：
```
{:height="700px" width="400px"}
```

参见: [Inserting Images in Markdown Jekyll Posts](http://dev-notes.eu/2016/01/images-in-kramdown-jekyll/)
